Back Workout

Sets & Reps: 4 sets of 6-10 reps each

Sets & Reps: 4 sets of 10-15 reps each

Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets

Rest: 60 seconds between sets

Grooming Tips for Men

Good grooming improves confidence and appearance. Here's how to groom yourself effectively:

  • Maintain a clean haircut, visit barber regularly.
  • Trim facial hair neatly or shave regularly.
  • Keep nails clean and trimmed.
  • Use deodorant and perfume moderately.
  • Moisturize skin and protect with sunscreen.
  • Dress neatly and keep clothes clean.
Eye Care Tips: Reduce Eye Bags and Sunken Eyes
  • Get consistent, quality sleep (7-9 hours per night).
  • Stay well-hydrated throughout the day.
  • Apply cold compresses or chilled cucumber slices to the eyes for 10-15 minutes.
  • Use eye creams containing caffeine or retinol to improve circulation and skin elasticity.
  • Maintain a healthy diet rich in antioxidants, vitamins C and K.
  • Reduce salt intake to minimize fluid retention.
  • Protect skin from sun damage using sunscreen and sunglasses.
  • If persistent, consult a dermatologist for treatments like fillers or laser therapy.
